# Guess The Number
<h2>Description:</h2>
This is a simple game made by Python console, but you have to use your mind and your luck to play it!<br>
<h2>Game Rule:</h2>
-Your original score is 7500. You have to get 10000 to win the game.<br>
-Type your number and use the suggestion from the game: "Greater" or "Less".<br>
-If the number is wrong, you will lose your score equals to that number.<br>
-When you got the number, your score will increase by your luck.
